Stkcd [Stock Code] - Stock code released by the exchange of the listed company
ShortName_EN [Stock Short Name] - Stock Short Name released by the exchange of the listed company.
Accper [Ending Date of Statistics] - Date of accounting statement, denoted by 10 characters, e.g., 1999-12-31.
Typrep [Statement Type] - The listed company's financial reports are consolidated statements or parent statements. A=Consolidated Statements, B=Parent Statements.
B002000000 [Net Profit] - The net profit realized by an enterprise. Used from 1990 onwards. Some companies did not have this item before 1994.
