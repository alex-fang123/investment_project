Stkcd [Stock Code] - Codes released by the Shanghai and Shenzhen Stock Exchanges
Trdmnt [Trading Month] - Denoted by YYYY-MM
Opndt [Day of Opening Price of Month] - Denoted by DD, the first trading day in the month, that is, the day when monthly opening price occurs; “DD” = No trading in the month
Mopnprc [Monthly Opening Price] - Opening price on the first trading day of the month
Clsdt [Day of Closing Price of Month] - Denoted by DD, the last trading day in the month, that is, the day when monthly closing price occurs; “DD” = No trading in the month
Mclsprc [Monthly Closing Price] - Closing price on the last trading day of the month
Msmvttl [Total Market Value] - The total number of share multiplied by its monthly closing price. A Share: CNY, SSE B share: USD, SZSE B Share: HKD.
Mretwd [Monthly Return With Cash Dividend Reinvested] - Please refer to “How Returns Are Calculated”
Mretnd [Monthly Return Without Cash Dividend Reinvested] - Please refer to “How Returns Are Calculated”
